Stable Quality for Environmental Protection Solutions

RO Membrane

Reverse osmosis (RO) membranes feature high rejection rates for impurities, selective permeability for water molecules, efficient pressure-driven operation, chemical resistance, and a long operational lifespan, making them ideal for various water purification applications.

UF Membrane

Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes effectively remove particles, bacteria, and viruses while allowing essential minerals to pass through, by utilizing a low-pressure process with high permeability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for both drinking water treatment and industrial applications.

Domestic RO membrane

Domestic RO membrane elements are spiral wound, composite polyamide membrane elements for Tap Water and Well Water treatment in low salinity application.
